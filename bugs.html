A crude way to report a bug is to email a report to Dan Kelley@Dal.Ca. However, people who are comfortable with web interfaces are highly encouraged to instead use the development site to report bugs, because that makes it much easier to track the bug fix. The development site also lists previous bug reports, so it can be a good place to look to see if your bug has already been reported.
